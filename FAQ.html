Frequently Asked Questions

How do you find my match?

It's that simple.

When you buy a partner, we put you in a waiting list, where we wait for a secondary person that matches the description of your perfect partner. We then send you a message with the details of your match, and you can start chatting with them. The rest is history.

What if I dont like them?

It's that simple too.

Firstly, we ask you to communicate to your partner about the issue. You can then contact us so we can unmatch you, and we'll start looking for a new match.

What if I can't find a match?

Even simpler.

If you can't find a match and feel like this is not going to work out, we will issue a refund, where we will give you all of your money back in a 7 business days window, excluding the service fees.
